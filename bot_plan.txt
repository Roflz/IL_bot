Questing flowthrough:
1. Equip combat items, set autocast to wind strike
2. withdraw from bank: 1 egg, 1 bucket of milk, 2 pot of flour, coins, mind runes, 1 spade, beads for imp catcher, 2 bucket of water, 1 cadava berries, 1 tinderbox, 1 knife, 1 redberries, 1 yellow dye, 3 balls of wool, 1 ashes, 1 soft clay
3. Start demon slayer, get the first 2 keys that are in varrock.
4. Do romeo and juliet
5. home teleport to lumbridge
6. do cooks assistant
7. Start Rune mysteries
8. start restless ghost
9. start x marks the spot
10. Start Prince Ali Rescue, talk to osman for first step of quest
11. do first steps of x marks the spot in lumbridge
12. do first steps of restless ghost, up until you have to go to wizards tower, do misthalin mystery on the way
13. Navigate to the wizards tower, do imp catcher, grab the skull for restless ghost, and talk to archmage sedridor for rune mysteries to get the research package
14. Go to draynor village, dig in next 2 spots for x marks the spot, do steps for prince ali rescue up to making the key mould.
15. Bank, grab 25 bones, go back to wizards tower and exchange them for the 3rd demon slayer key
16. Go to draynor village, buy chronicle from draynor and buy pages to put in it, equip it. Buy cursed goblin staff.
17. Start Vampyre slayer
18. Bank, grab Ancient casket, white apron, coins, spade, 10 bananas, onion, eye of newt, cooked meat, mind runes
19. Do Pirates Treasure quest up until you get the key
20. Talk to Veos to complete X marks the spot
21. Use Lamp on Prayer
22. Start Corsair Curse quest by talking to captain tok
23. Go to rimmington, complete witches potion quest.
24. Go to Falador
25. Bank, grab mind runes, coins, 6 clay, 4 copper, 2 iron ore, 3 goblin mail, 1 orangge dye, 1 blue dye, 1 bronze med helm, 1 iron chainbody, 1 cabbage, and rest of inventory fill with trout
26. Go to white knights castle, start black knights fortress and knights sword quests
27. Go complete dorics quest, and goblin diplomacy, then do black knights fortress, don't go back to falador to complete black knights fortress yet, talk to willow to start below ice mountain
28. Go to barbarian village and complete next step of Below Ice Mountain
29. Go to Edgeville. Talk to marley for next step of below ice mountain
30. Go to Varrock. Withdraw 1 cooked meat, 1 bread, 1 knife, 3 demon slayer keys, 1 chest key, 1 beer, rest of inventory take food.
31. Go to Varrock Castle, talk to sir prysin to get silverlight
32. Go to castle library, talk to reldo for knights sword quest
33. Talk to Aris to get the incantation for demon slayer
34. Go to Aubury in south east varrock for rune mysteries to get the notes
35. Go to Blue Moon Inn in Varrock, do the next steps for Below Ice Mountain, Pirates Treasure, and Vampyre Slayer
36. Go Kill Delrith
37. Home Tele to lumbridge
38. Bank, grab coins, skull, bronze bar, key print, 20 balls of wool
39. Go finish restless ghost
40. Go talk to osman in al kharid
41. Go to draynor and complete sheep shearer on the way
42. Bank at draynor, grab wig, paste, rope, 3 beers, pink skirt, research notes
43. Go to wizards tower basement and talk to sedridor to finish rune mysteries
44. Go talk to leela and then go to draynor jail to break ali out of there for Prince Ali Rescue
45. Bank at draynor, deposit all, withdraw mind runes, amulet of magic (equip it), stake, garlic, hammer, fish food, poison, spade, food for fight againt vampyre
46. go to draynor manor, talk to veronica outside to start ernest the chicken on ur way in
47. go into draynor manor, go kill the vampyre to finish vampyre slayer.
48. Complete Enerest the chicken
49. Exit Draynor Manor, go bank at Draynor, withdraw 1 redberry pie, 2 iron bar, any pickaxe, rest food for Corsair Curse boss.
50. Go finish corsair curse
51. Then go finish knights sword.
52. Go back to falador white knights castle to finish black knights fortress and knights sword.
53. Grab a spade and finish pirates treasure by digging at the X in falador park
54. Bank, withdraw steak sandwich, coins, mind runes, pickaxe, fill rest of inventory with food. go finish below Ice Mountain
55. Lumbridge home teleport -> go to al kharid palace to copmlete prince ali rescue.
And then we are done with all quests except Shield of Arrav and Dragon Slayer
